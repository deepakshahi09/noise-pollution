Loud speaker noise

Loud noise from speakers can be caused by several factors

Here are some solutions to fix the problem:

Troubleshoot the hardware problem
Break the electrical ground loop
Avoid frequency interference
Update your audio driver
Configure audio settings in computer
Check all connections between the speakers and the audio source
Keep your speaker away from electronic components such as TVs or computers if possible
Use a ground loop isolator
